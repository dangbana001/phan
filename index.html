Scene 01 — Profile

About

I'm Phantasie, a cinematographer and front-end developer based in Lagos, Nigeria. My work sits at the intersection of two disciplines that are rarely combined — the visual instinct of a director of photography, and the technical precision of a developer.

On set, I compose frames, direct light, and cut footage into stories that move people — promos, events, and short-form content built for how audiences actually watch today. Behind the screen, I build the websites and interfaces that give that work a home, from marketplace platforms to portfolio experiences.

Every project I take on is treated the same way: frame it with intention, build it to last.
